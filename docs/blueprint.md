# **App Name**: SOL Theory Reimagined

## Core Features:

- Informative Home Page: Display key information about SOL Theory and its mission on the home page using existing screenshots and OCR content. Ensure key sections like 'Who We Are', 'What We're Up To', 'How We Do Things', and 'Vision & Goals' are prominently featured.
- Interactive 3D Carousel: A carousel of images rendered in 3D.
- Subscription Prompt: Integrate a subscription prompt within the 'Want In?' section for users to show their interest and subscribe. Save subscription details for potential email marketing or outreach.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#4C2882), evocative of wisdom, royalty, and self-improvement.
- Background color: Very dark desaturated purple (#1A121F), close to black to highlight the content.
- Accent color: Soft lavender (#BCA0DC), provides a gentle highlight and modern feel against the dark background.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', for headings and titles, lending an elegant, fashionable feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif pairing that's modern, neutral, and readable in longer sections of text
- Use clean, minimalist icons with a white or light lavender stroke to represent various concepts and categories within SOL Theory.
- Design a layout that presents information in a structured manner, using distinct sections separated by visual cues. Maintain generous spacing to avoid clutter.
- Incorporate subtle animations on scroll or hover effects to enhance the interactive experience without being distracting. For example, gentle fading effects or parallax scrolling can be employed.
